COVID-19

Coronavurus disease (COVID-19) is an infectious disease caused by a new virus

The disease cause respiratory illness like the flu with symptoms such as a cough, fever and in more svere cases, difficulty in breathing. You can protect yourself by washing your hands frequently, avoiding touching your face, and avoiding close contact (1 meter or 3 feet) with people who are unwell

How it spreads?

Coronavirus disease spreads primarily through contact with an infected person when they cough or sneeze. it also spreads when a person touches a surface or an object that has the virus on it, then touches there eyes nose and mouth
